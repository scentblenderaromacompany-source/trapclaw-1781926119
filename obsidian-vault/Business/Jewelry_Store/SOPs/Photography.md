# Jewelry Photography SOP

## Purpose
Consistent, high-quality product photos that maximize perceived value and minimize returns.

## Equipment
- Camera: iPhone 15 Pro+ or DSLR
- Lighting: 2 softbox lights + ring light
- Background: white poster board + black velvet
- Props: measuring card, coin for scale
- Macro lens or clip-on macro for phone

## Lighting Setup
```
        [Softbox 1]
            \
             \  45°
              \
    [Subject] ---- [Softbox 2]
              /
             /  45°
            /
        [Ring Light - overhead]
```

- Color temp: 5500K (daylight)
- No direct flash — causes harsh reflections on metal
- Diffuse everything

## Shot List (Per Item)

### Required (5 minimum)
| # | Shot | Purpose |
|---|------|---------|
| 1 | Front hero | Main listing image |
| 2 | Back/detail | Shows construction |
| 3 | Hallmark close-up | Authenticity proof |
| 4 | Scale reference | Size context |
| 5 | Lifestyle/worn | Emotional connection |

### Bonus (if time allows)
| # | Shot | Purpose |
|---|------|---------|
| 6 | Box/packaging | Premium feel |
| 7 | Side angle | Depth/dimension |
| 8 | Macro stone detail | Quality evidence |
| 9 | Movement shot | Shows how it catches light |

## Camera Settings
- **Phone:** Portrait mode, 2x zoom, tap to focus on hallmark
- **DSLR:** f/8-f/11, ISO 100, tripod mandatory for macro
- Shoot in RAW when possible, export as JPEG/WebP

## Post-Processing
1. Crop to square (1:1) for listings
2. Adjust white balance (background should be pure white #FFFFFF)
3. Slight sharpening — don't overdo it
4. Remove dust/scratches with clone tool
5. Export: 2000x2000px minimum, sRGB color profile

## Common Mistakes
- ❌ Yellow/warm white background (looks cheap)
- ❌ Fingers visible holding the item
- ❌ Blurry hallmark shots (use tripod + timer)
- ❌ Over-editing (color shifting gold looks fake)
- ❌ Missing scale reference (buyers can't judge size)

## File Organization
```
photos/
  {date}_{item-id}/
    raw/          ← unedited originals
    edited/       ← processed finals
    listing/      ← cropped & exported for upload
```
