# Jewelry Pricing SOP

## Purpose
Consistent, profitable pricing across all channels while remaining competitive.

## Pricing Formula
```
Base Price = (Material Value × Multiplier) + Labor + Overhead

Material Value:
  Gold = Weight (g) × Purity × Spot Price
  Silver = Weight (g) × 0.925 × Spot Price
  Platinum = Weight (g) × Spot Price

Multipliers:
  Scrap/wholesale → 1.0-1.5x
  Vintage/antique → 2-4x
  Designer/branded → 3-8x
  Signed pieces → 5-10x
```

## Price Tiers

| Tier | Range | Strategy |
|------|-------|----------|
| Entry | $25-75 | Volume play, fast turnover |
| Mid | $75-300 | Sweet spot, best margins |
| Premium | $300-1000 | Fewer sales, higher per-unit profit |
| Luxury | $1000+ | Consignment or specialty channels |

## Channel Adjustments

| Channel | Adjustment | Reason |
|---------|-----------|--------|
| eBay | +15% (offer space) | Buyers expect to "win" a deal |
| Etsy | Base price | Handmade/vintage buyers pay fair |
| Shopify | +10% | Direct traffic = higher intent |
| Poshmark | +20% (share-friendly) | 20% commission built in |
| In-person | -10% | Relationship value, no shipping risk |

## Spot Price Tracking
- Check gold/silver spot daily (kitco.com or goldprice.org)
- Update pricing weekly for high-volume items
- Lock in material cost at time of acquisition, not listing

## Discount Rules
- Never discount more than 20% from listed price
- Bundle discounts: 10% off 2+ items
- Loyalty: 15% repeat customer discount
- Seasonal: Black Friday max 25%, holiday sales max 15%

## Price Anchoring Tactics
1. Show "compare at" price (retail replacement value)
2. Original retail receipt if available
3. "Last one at this price" for unique items
4. Free shipping as implicit discount (raise price, offer "free" shipping)

## Review Cadence
- **Weekly:** Check top 20 listings for competitiveness
- **Monthly:** Full catalog review, adjust stale listings
- **Quarterly:** Reassess tier thresholds vs actual margins

## Red Flags
- ⚠️ Selling below material value (unless intentional loss leader)
- ⚠️ Items sitting 60+ days without price adjustment
- ⚠️ More than 30% of sales at discount (pricing too high initially)
