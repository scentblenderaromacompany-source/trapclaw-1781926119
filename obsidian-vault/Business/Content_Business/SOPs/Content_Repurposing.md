# Content Repurposing SOP

## Purpose
Maximize content ROI by systematically repurposing one piece of content into 10+ outputs across platforms.

## The Content Pyramid

```
          [1 Long-Form Piece]
          (YouTube video, blog post, podcast)
               /     |     \
              /      |      \
        [3-5 Short Clips]  [Blog Post]  [Newsletter]
           /    |    \          |              |
          /     |     \        |              |
   [Social Posts] [Quotes] [Carousel]   [Thread]
```

## Repurposing Workflow

### Step 1: Create Core Content
- One 10-15 min YouTube video OR
- One 2000+ word blog post OR
- One 30+ min podcast episode

### Step 2: Extract Shorts (from video)
1. Identify 3-5 key moments (60-90 sec each)
2. Cut with captions
3. Add hooks specific to each platform
4. Schedule across 1-2 weeks

### Step 3: Written Derivatives
From the same content:
- **Blog post:** Expand or transcribe with SEO优化
- **Twitter/X thread:** 5-10 key takeaways
- **LinkedIn post:** Professional angle, lessons learned
- **Instagram carousel:** Visual summary (5-10 slides)
- **Newsletter section:** Personal take + link

### Step 4: Visual Derivatives
- Quote cards from key statements
- Infographic from data/step sequences
- Meme templates if tone fits
- Pinterest pins (vertical graphics)

## Platform-Specific Adaptations

| Platform | Content Type | Tone | Length |
|----------|-------------|------|--------|
| TikTok | Short video | Casual, high energy | 15-60 sec |
| Instagram | Reels + Carousel | Aspirational, visual | 30-90 sec / 5-10 slides |
| YouTube | Long + Shorts | Informative, personality | 8-15 min / 30-60 sec |
| Twitter/X | Thread | Punchy, opinionated | 5-10 tweets |
| LinkedIn | Article + Post | Professional, insightful | 200-500 words |
| Newsletter | Email | Personal, exclusive | 500-1000 words |
| Pinterest | Pin | Aesthetic, aspirational | Static or video |

## Content Calendar Integration
- Week 1: Film core video
- Week 1-2: Release shorts (2-3 per week)
- Week 2: Publish blog post
- Week 2-3: Social carousel + thread
- Week 3: Newsletter feature
- Ongoing: Reshare top performers quarterly

## Automation Opportunities
- Transcription: Whisper or Descript
- Clip detection: Opus Clip or similar
- Caption generation: Auto-captions + manual review
- Scheduling: Buffer, Later, or Hootsuite
- Cross-posting: Repurpose.io

## Metrics per Derivative
Track which repurposed format drives the most:
- Traffic back to core content
- New followers
- Engagement
- Conversions (email signups, sales)

## Quality Rules
- Never copy-paste across platforms — always adapt
- Each platform version must stand alone
- Don't repurpose everything — pick highest-value content
- Minimum 1 week gap between resharing same content
