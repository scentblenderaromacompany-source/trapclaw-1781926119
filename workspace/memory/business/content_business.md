# Content Business — Context File

---

## Mission
Build an autonomous content engine that generates, distributes, and monetizes content across multiple platforms with minimal human intervention.

---

## Business Model
- **Primary:** Content-as-a-service (automated content for clients)
- **Secondary:** Template/prompt libraries for creators
- **Tertiary:** Automation tools and workflows

---

## Target Revenue: $10k MRR by December 2026

### Revenue Streams
1. **Managed Content Packages** — $2-5k/mo per client
   - Social media content (AI-generated, human-reviewed)
   - Blog posts + SEO optimization
   - Email sequences
   
2. **Template Libraries** — $500-2k/mo
   - Video script templates
   - Image generation prompts
   - Content repurposing frameworks
   
3. **Automation Tools** — $1-3k/mo
   - Content scheduling systems
   - Multi-platform publishing
   - Analytics dashboards

### Content Types
1. **Short-form video** (TikTok, Reels, Shorts) — 15-60 sec
2. **Long-form video** (YouTube) — 8-15 min
3. **Blog posts** — 1000-2500 words
4. **Social media** — Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Instagram
5. **Email sequences** — Nurture, sales, onboarding
6. **Audio** — Podcast clips, music beds
