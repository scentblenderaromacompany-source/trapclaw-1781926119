## Long-Term Vision (2027+)

### The Autonomous Business
1. **Content Engine** — Generates, publishes, and optimizes content across all platforms without human intervention. Revenue from templates, tools, and managed services.

2. **Jewelry Intelligence** — AI evaluates incoming pieces, suggests pricing, writes listings, manages inventory, and identifies sourcing opportunities. Robert just buys and ships.

3. **Agent Swarm** — Specialized agents collaborate on complex tasks. Research agent finds trends → Content agent creates assets → Executive agent orchestrates distribution.

4. **Knowledge Compounding** — Every interaction, decision, and outcome feeds back into the memory system. The AI gets smarter about Robert's preferences, business patterns, and market dynamics.

### The End State
Robert's role: Vision, relationships, big decisions.
AI's role: Everything else.

## Anti-Goals (What We're NOT Building)

- ❌ Another SaaS that requires constant maintenance
- ❌ Content that feels AI-generated (quality threshold: indistinguishable from human)
- ❌ Systems that break when Robert travels
- ❌ Complexity for complexity's sake — simplicity is a feature
